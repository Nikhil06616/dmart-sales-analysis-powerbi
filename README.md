# dmart-sales-analysis-powerbi  
# 🛒 DMart Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

**DMart Analytics: Sales & Profit Performance Dashboard**
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales, profit trends, customer behavior, and regional performance for a retail business.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales and profit performance across regions, categories, and time. It helps identify key trends, top-performing areas, and business opportunities for better decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Data visualization and dashboard creation
* 📂 **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Measures and calculations
* 📝 **Data Modeling** – Table relationships and data structuring
* 📁 **File Formats** – `.pbix`, `.png`

---

## 📂 Data Source

**Source:** KAGGLE - dmart grocery sales
            (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/nikolaireeds/dmart-grocery-sales)

**Dataset includes:**

* Order details (Order ID, Sales, Profit, Discount)
* Customer data (Name, City, Region)
* Product categories (Snacks, Beverages, Food Grains, etc.)
* Time data (Month, Year)

---

## ✨ Features & Highlights

### 🔍 Business Problem

Retail businesses handle large volumes of data, making it difficult to answer questions like:

* Which region generates the highest profit?
* Which category performs best?
* Who are the top customers?
* What are the monthly sales trends?

---

### 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

* Track overall **sales and profit performance**
* Enable **region-wise and category-wise analysis**
* Identify **top customers and cities**
* Support **data-driven decision making**

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 🔢 Key KPIs

* **Total Profit:** 3.75M
* **Total Sales:** 15M
* **Average Discount:** 0.23
* **Total Orders:** 9994

---

### 📅 Monthly Sales Trend

* Sales vary across months
* Peak performance observed in **September, November, December**

---

### 🌍 Regional Performance

* **West region** generates the highest sales and profit
* Followed by East and Central regions

---

### 🥧 Category Analysis

* Categories like **Snacks, Beverages, and Food Grains** contribute significantly
* Helps identify top-performing product segments

---

### 🏙️ City-Level Insights

Top cities by sales:

* Kanyakumari
* Vellore
* Bodi

---

### 🧑‍💼 Top Customers

* Identifies highest revenue-generating customers
* Useful for retention and marketing strategies

---

### 📦 Sales vs Profit by Category

* Highlights categories with high sales but lower profit
* Helps in improving pricing and discount strategies

---

## 💡 Business Impact

* 📈 Identify high-performing regions and months
* 🎯 Improve targeted marketing strategies
* 📦 Optimize product category performance
* 🌍 Support regional expansion decisions
* 💰 Enhance profit through better discount management
